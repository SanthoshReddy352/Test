## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I register for multiple events?
Yes! You can register for as many events as you'd like.

### Can I edit my registration after submitting?
No, currently you cannot edit submissions. If you made a mistake and your registration is rejected, you can re-register with correct information.

### What if my registration is rejected?
You're welcome to register again with updated information. Make sure to address any potential issues from your previous application.

### How long does approval take?
Approval time varies by event. Event organizers typically review applications within 24-48 hours.

### I didn't receive an approval/rejection email. What should I do?
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify you used the correct email address
3. Contact the event organizers through the contact form

### Can I cancel my registration?
Currently, there's no self-service cancellation. Contact the event organizers if you need to withdraw.

### What happens after my registration is approved?
You'll receive a confirmation email and should watch for further communications from event organizers about:
- Event schedule
- Platform/location details
- Pre-event requirements
- Day-of instructions
